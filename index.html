    • KSP (KANLUNGAN SHELTER PHILIPPINES) is a temporary shelter for dogs, cats and other animals rescued from cruelty or neglect with the goal of rehabilitating and placing them in loving homes.
    KANLUNGAN is a registered non-profit organization which is dedicated to the protection and the promotion of humane treatment of all animals.


    MISSION

    The Kanlungan Shelter Philippines exists to prevent cruelty, alleviate pain, fear and suffering in animals and to promote a society based on humane principles.
    KSP is a credible nationwide non-profit organization, composed of a network of committed, compassionate and trustworthy individuals and institutions that initiates and leads in the promotion of animal welfare, and the protection of all animals. KSP envisions a nation that respects animals, practices responsible pet ownership, and protects wildlife.

    VISION


    Please donate and help save our shelter animals

    Every year, the KSP Animal Rehabilitation Center cares for around 300 dogs and cats rescued from cruelty or neglect. The costs of treating and rehabilitating these animals, as well as running the shelter and clinic, are funded solely by donations.

    Rescue, Rehab & Adoption

    The KANLUNGAN shelter is intended for animals involved in cases of cruelty or neglect. We provide physical and behavioral rehabilitation for these rescued animals to be able to find them forever homes, while their cases are pending in court.
